Will you arrange for a driver's license for me?

Yes, we start processing your license right from the day we present you to DVLA for your learner's license.

Is it compulsory that I go to DVLA for the tests?

Yes, It is only DVLA that can issue a valid driving license for students drivers, Therefore, the theory and practical tests are compulsory.

What is the duration of the program?

The regular package lasts 6 weeks, the express lasts 3 weeks and polishing lasts 4 weeks.
